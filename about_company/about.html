about company

Alpha Distribution LLC is a distributor of IT equipment, IT solutions and services. General partner of ABRIS Distribution in Georgia.

We use the maximum range of modern information technologies and management approaches to solve the problems of our partners. Our work increases the efficiency of the business and creates a solid foundation for its further development.

our mission

Raise information technology standards and livelihoods in the region by promoting innovative products and solutions to markets combined with superior customer support.

company vision

Expanding the boundaries of our presence, promote high technologies and raise the level of technical knowledge of the society, contributing to progress in the countries of the Region.
